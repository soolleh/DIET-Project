Subject : Last date for the receiptof fee for the examination of class 5th to 9th (T1 & T2) session 2017
It is notified for the information of all concerned that last date for the receipt of fee for the examination of class 5th to 9th (T1 & T2) session 2017 is 6th April 2017 for Complex Heads.All the students of Government as well as Private recognized schools are directed to deposit their fee through their schools in the concerned Complex Heads @ Rs 180/= per student for class 5th and Rs 280/= for class 6th,7th,8th, and 9th

Late fee shall be charged as detailed below: For the first count of 10 days @Rs 50/= per candidate shall be charged and for the second count of 7 days @ Rs 100/= per candidate.Rs 10/= per day per candidate shall be charged beyond the period of 17 days
